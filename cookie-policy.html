Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2, 2026

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to store information that can be read by a web server in the domain that placed the cookie. They help websites remember preferences and improve user experience.

2. How we use cookies

  • Essential cookies: necessary for core site functions like shopping cart and account login.
  • Performance cookies: collect anonymous information about how visitors use the site, e.g., pages visited and errors.
  • Functional cookies: remember choices you make to provide enhanced features.
  • Advertising/targeting cookies: used to deliver relevant ads and measure campaign effectiveness (third-party providers may set these).

3. Third-party cookies

We may allow third-party services (analytics and advertising partners) to set cookies on our site. These providers have their own privacy and cookie practices.

4. Managing cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking certain cookies may affect site functionality. For more details, visit your browser's help pages.

5. Changes to this Cookie Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates the latest revision.

6. Contact

Questions about cookies can be sent to: privacy@ellamilla.example.
